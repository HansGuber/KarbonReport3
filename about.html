Who We Are

We're a group of financial experts, environmental scientist and compliance professionals creating compliance tools for the carbon economy.

Karbon Reporter provides a software that allows Malaysian exporters to satisfy the Carbon Border Adjustment Mechanims (CBAM) reporting requirements as required by the European Union. Manage all European importers by providing them the information that they need whilst maintaining the confidenitality of your business operations..

User friendly & Completeness of Data - You enter only the information that is required to complete the calculations required by your customer. Nothing more, nothing less.

Data Integrity - Your business data, processes and information is secure with you. The system only submits what is required. You remain in control of what your customers see.

Automatic Report Generation - Our software automatically populates the report from the submitted data. This report contains all the necessary information for direct submission to each individual customer.

As a startup company we are able to respond quickly to the changes that are taking place within the carbon economy. Whilst things in other jurisdictions are gathering steam and carbon tax is being implemented in stages, other countries are taking a more wait and see approach. This however will not stop the envitable as we head towards Net Zero and achieving targets set at the Paris Accord. If you're keen to save the planet and join a fast moving industry with long term prospect, please join our team - we are always on the look out for brilliant people.
