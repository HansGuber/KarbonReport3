Karbon Report

Your reporting platform for all your Carbon Border Adjustment Mechanism (CBAM) needs.

Our platform is a compliance platform to help companies report on their embedded carbon content in their products. It is a legal requirement of the European Union to begin reporting on your carbon footprint as a basis for eventually paying Carbon Tax.
